Our Story

LearnIspire was founded in 2022 by a group of educators and technologists who saw the potential for technology to transform how students learn and how teachers teach.

After years of working in traditional educational settings, our founders realized that many students were not getting the personalized attention they needed to excel. Meanwhile, talented teachers were often constrained by rigid systems that didn't allow them to fully apply their skills.
