1. Introduction

LearnIspire ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at learnispire.com and use our platform and services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using our platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Respond to surveys
  • Make payments

The personal information we collect may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Education information
  • Payment information
  • Profile pictures
2.2. Information Collected Automatically

When you access our platform, we may automatically collect certain information including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and activities on our platform
  • Time and date of your visit
  • Time spent on specific pages
  • Referring website addresses
2.3. Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party services like Google, WhatsApp, and PayU when you choose to link these services to our platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our platform

  • Monitoring and analyzing usage patterns
  • Protecting our platform against fraudulent or illegal activity

We may share your information with third parties in certain circumstances, including:

4.1. Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party vendors, service providers, and other contractors who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, data analysis, email delivery, and hosting services.

4.4. With Your Consent

We may share your information with your consent or at your direction.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Our platform is intended for use by children with parental consent. We collect information from children under 18 only with verifiable parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, which may include:

You can update your account information and preferences by logging into your account settings or by contacting us directly.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Revised" date, and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy frequently to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
