Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some common questions

Getting started is easy! Simply register for an account, verify your phone number through WhatsApp, complete your profile by selecting your grade and board, choose your subjects, set your availability, and request a demo class. After experiencing a demo class, you can decide to continue with regular classes.

We have a rigorous selection process for our teachers. All teachers must have proper qualifications and experience in their subject areas. They go through an application review, background check, and demonstration classes before being approved to teach on our platform.

You can set your availability based on when you're free to attend classes. We require a minimum of 3 slots per week. Once you've set your availability, we'll match you with appropriate teachers and create a monthly schedule that respects your time preferences.

We understand that things come up. You can request cancellations through your dashboard at least 24 hours in advance. We'll work to reschedule the class at a time that works for both you and the teacher. Frequent cancellations may affect class scheduling, so we encourage maintaining a consistent schedule.

Payments are processed monthly through our secure payment gateway powered by PayU. You'll receive a detailed breakdown of charges before making any payment. We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking options.
